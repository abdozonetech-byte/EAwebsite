Upgrade 15 — Mobile Menu Background Cleanup

Scope:
- Mobile offcanvas menu only.
- Desktop design, SEO, security, tracking, lead page, articles and homepage sections were not changed.

What changed:
- Removed the heavy pure-white drawer feeling from the mobile menu.
- Mobile menu now sits on a soft Elboubakry-style background with subtle grid/radial accents.
- Offcanvas internal sections are transparent, so they feel directly placed on the background.
- Menu links, about text, CTA, contact area and icons stay readable and aligned.
- CTA keeps the same brand style and remains fully contained.
- Overlay remains dark/blurred outside the menu for focus.

Testing recommended:
- Open homepage at 360px, 390px and 430px widths.
- Open/close the mobile menu.
- Confirm no horizontal overflow.
- Confirm the CTA fits.
- Confirm text wraps correctly.
- Confirm desktop navbar is unchanged.
